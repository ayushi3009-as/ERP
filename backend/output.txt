Manufacturing ERP — Specification
Internal Use Only — No Customer, Sales, Vendor, or Warehouse Modules
Purpose
This ERP is built solely for the company's internal manufacturing operations. It will be used only by company employees. The entire production flow is managed through Design Number, Lot Number, Barcode/QR Code, and Employee Tracking. Every product has its own Design, every production batch has its own Lot Number, and every Lot can be scanned via Barcode/QR Code to instantly track its current production stage, size, quantity, and progress. There is no Customer, Sales, Vendor, or Warehouse module of any kind, since this system is customized purely for internal manufacturing management.
